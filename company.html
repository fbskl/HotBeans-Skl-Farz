About Hot Beans Web
Hot Beans Web is a modern web development company focused on helping new developers begin their careers in the tech industry. We work with real clients, build real projects, and support trainees as they grow their skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern frameworks. Our goal is to create a supportive environment where new developers can learn, collaborate, and gain the experience they need to succeed.
What We Offer

Real Projects

Trainees work on real client websites, gaining hands on experience that prepares them for real industry roles.

Mentor Support

Our experienced developers guide trainees through code reviews, pair programming, and technical challenges.

Career Progression

Many trainees progress into junior developer roles within 6–12 months of joining Hot Beans Web.
